Multi National Corporation


A multinational corporation (MNC) is a company that has business operations in at least one country other than its home country and generates revenue beyond its borders. Chances are the clothes you're wearing, the smartphone in your pocket, and the transportation you take to work all have one thing in common: they were likely manufactured by an MNC, as are 90% of American imports.
